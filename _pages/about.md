I am a PhD researcher working on atmospheric composition, aerosols, and air quality, with a focus on Sub-Saharan Africa.

My research uses satellite observations, reanalysis data, and machine learning models to estimate air pollution and assess its impacts on human health and climate.

I am particularly interested in ozone formation, biomass burning emissions, and regional transport of pollutants across Africa.

## Research Interests
- Air pollution modeling (PM2.5, O3)
- Biomass burning emissions
- Satellite remote sensing
- Machine learning for atmospheric science
- Health impacts of air pollution
